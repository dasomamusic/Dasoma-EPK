About Dasoma (Avery)

Dasoma blends immersive sound design with organic textures to create emotionally rich, bass-driven music inspired by artists like Charlesthefirst, Of The Trees, and Chmura. Rooted in atmospheric storytelling and downtempo rhythms, his project bridges nature and sound—drawing listeners into more introspective and somatic experiences. With a growing presence in the experimental bass scene, Dasoma continues to craft deep, genre-defying journeys that resonate beyond the dancefloor.

At the heart of Dasoma’s work is a love for contrast—dark and light, chaos and calm, digital and natural. Field recordings, melodic layers, and deep bass come together to form a space where people can feel fully present, whether they’re moving or still. Each track is meant to hold space for reflection, offering something more than just a drop—it’s about telling a story without words.

With releases beginning to gain traction and live sets becoming more immersive, Dasoma is carving out his own space in the scene. Whether it’s through late-night forest sets or studio sessions rooted in solitude, he’s focused on building a sound that feels honest and grounded. This is music for feeling, not just listening.
